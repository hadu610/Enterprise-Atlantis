Step C · autonomy

How much do you want to gate?

Pick the autonomy mode per agent. Drafting means the agent never executes — you review every output. Approval means the agent produces a Resolution Plan and waits for your sign-off. Silent means the agent executes immediately and surfaces every action in the Live Activity Stream with a 30-second undo. Read the spec →

Platform-floor guardrails

Hard limits that survive every mode.

These thresholds always require human approval, regardless of the autonomy mode you pick. You can tighten any number; you cannot disable the floor itself.

On activation, the Atlantis Manager will open with your picks pre-loaded. You'll see the full Resolution Plan for the company spin-up before any state is created.
