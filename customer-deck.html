How control works

You set the autonomy. Evidence earns the next step.

Every customer starts in Draft mode — agents draft, humans approve. Move to Read, then Approve, then Auto — only when your Trust Score Dashboard says you're ready.

01

Draft

Agents draft, you approve every action. Zero risk.

02

Read

Agents read & summarise; humans still write.

03

Approve

Agents act with your approval queue.

04

Auto

Agents act within policy; humans handle exceptions.

Delete and financial actions always require a human — hard rule, never configurable.

Speaker notes. "This is why our customers don't cancel. You decide when to give the agent more autonomy. The platform brings evidence; you bring the decision."
